28 - Building an Interview Bible

October 16th 2023


Let Derek Be Your Guide

Didn't make as much progress today as I would've liked, but I did start working on a new section of Coachable work. I've been adapting the videos I've watched of programmers doing live-code interviews into a series of steps to take.

Essentially:

  1. Discuss with the interviewer the expected behavior in various scenarios, noting them down
  2. Determine the data structure(s) for the tast
  3. Begin Coding helper classes, then functions, then the main class
  4. Once ready, turn the noted examples from step 1 into test cases, and iterate through edge cases
  5. Discuss runtime, memory, any weaknesses in the code due to assumptions and possible fixes


It's a light list but it gives me a good roadmap to follow while I'm under pressure in a timed or live scenario


Let's hope practice makes perfect.
